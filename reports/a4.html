What was our rush hour?

For this visualization we wanted to know when during the day we have the highest volume of in revenue and transactions We wanted to be able to target promotions to maximize revenue so with this we can see where to emphasize our focus. The height of the circles are how much revenue was acquired in that hour in the year and the size of the ball is the quantity of transactions in that hour for the year. The amount of transactions is significant because people can buy different things and different amount if things so the total revenue and total transactions aren't directly related.



One of the key findings from our data analysis is the identification of a rush hour at 11am, as well as high traffic at 10am and 12pm. This information is reflected in the graph that shows the revenue and number of transactions by hour. By incorporating this knowledge into our plans, we will be better able to serve our customers and optimize our operations. We are also considering ways to increase traffic during the slower times of the day, such as 2pm and 3pm.
